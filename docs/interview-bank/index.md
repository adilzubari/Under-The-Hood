# Interview Bank

Cross-cutting index of every Q&A across the site, organized two ways.

## How to study

The most useful study mode is **active recall**. Pick a topic page, read the question, *try to answer in your head or out loud*, then click to reveal. If you couldn't answer cleanly, mark the topic to revisit.

Two views into the same questions:

- **[By Difficulty](by-difficulty.md)** — easy / medium / hard. Build up.
- **[By Topic](by-topic.md)** — grouped by phase. Targeted refresh before a topic-focused interview.

## Tips

1. **Don't read answers first.** The reveal mechanism is the whole point.
2. **Speak answers aloud.** Surfaces the gaps in your understanding.
3. **Spaced repetition.** Re-visit a question 1 day, 3 days, 1 week later. The model answer matters less than the mental motion.
4. **Generate scenarios from each question.** "How would I see this in production?" Then check the [Scenarios Bank](../scenarios-bank/index.md).
